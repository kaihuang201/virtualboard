Welcome to VirtualBoard


What is VirtualBoard?

VirtualBoard is an online platform that lets you connect with your friends in a virtual tabletop environment. Simply click the button above, pick a name and color, and start playing!


How does it work?

The game will run in any modern web browser, and you can launch your own webserver by downloading the python source code. It uses BabylonJS to render the game on the front end, and Tornado to run the game server.


How to play

Pieces are added by moving your mouse to the far left side of the game screen to open the game menu. Pieces are moved by clicking and dragging them with your mouse. You can control your camera with WASD + QE and the mouse wheel. You can right click on pieces to bring up a menu with more options. Resizing pieces is done with the + and - keys, and rotating pieces is done with the R and T keys.
